Our Journey

The Story Behind
Sapna Foam & Furniture

From a modest beginning in 1980, Sapna Furniture has grown into a symbol of luxury, comfort, and unmatched craftsmanship.

What started as a passionate dream to provide unparalleled comfort to homes in Kot Addu has evolved into a trusted legacy. For over four decades, our commitment to quality materials, innovative designs, and customer satisfaction has remained unwavering. Every piece of furniture crafted at Sapna carries a story of dedication, hard work, and a vision to transform houses into beautiful homes.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about shopping with us.

Do you offer custom furniture design?
Yes! We completely understand that luxury is personal. If you have a specific design in mind, our master craftsmen can build bespoke furniture tailored to your exact dimensions, fabric choices, and material preferences.
What is your delivery range?
While our primary showroom is in Kot Addu, we safely deliver to 5 major surrounding cities. For specific delivery inquiries outside our standard zone, please contact our support team via WhatsApp.
Do your foam products come with a warranty?
Absolutely. As an authorized premium dealer, all original mattress and foam products sold at Sapna Furniture carry the official brand warranty, ranging from 10 to 15 years depending on the specific model.
How can I place an order online?
Our online ordering is seamless and personal. Simply browse our 'Explore' section, find your desired item, and click the 'Order via WhatsApp' button. This connects you directly to our sales team who will finalize the details and arrange delivery.

Shop FAQs

Do you customize wood furniture?

Yes, we offer complete customization for wood furniture. You can select the wood type (e.g., Sheesham, Keekar), polish color, fabric, and dimensions to match your vision perfectly.

What home decor items are available?

Besides premium furniture, we offer high-end branded mattresses, elegant curtains, luxury wall-arts, and complete drawing room accessories imported and locally crafted.

Are there ongoing sales or discounts?

We host seasonal and clearance sales periodically. Special bundle discounts are always available if you purchase complete bedroom sets or wedding packages.

Support FAQs

How do I track my order?

Once your order is confirmed, our dispatcher will send you a live tracking link via WhatsApp. You can also contact our support team at any time.

What is the return & exchange policy?

For custom-made orders, returns are only accepted in case of significant manufacturing defects reported within 3 days of delivery.

How can I contact support?

You can reach our dedicated support team directly by calling 0333 6005865 or via WhatsApp at 0313 6791333 during our business hours.
